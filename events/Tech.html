Boat-O-Float

RULES AND REGULATION-
 Each team shall have a maximum of 4 people
 The canoe/boat must be designed by a team of students pursuing Engineering program at any AICTE approved University/Engineering College in India.
 The foam work for casting the canoe cannot be prepared by professionals other than the team members. All works (design, construction, testing, etc.) must be performed by the team members.
 The length, width, and height of your canoe must be less than or equal to 800±10%mm, 300 ±10%mm, and 300 ±10% mm, respectively.
 The outside college teams have to bring their furnished boats on 23 rd March 2018 and for CGC students materials will be provided on 28 th Feb 2018 and they have to construct the boat within 5 days and rest time is given for curing. Student will work in concrete lab only.
 CGC TC will be providing material only to 5 teams on 3 rd March 2018.
 Concrete will be allowed to set and cure for 21 days and testing would be done after 21 days only i.e. on 23 rd March 2018.
 The teams will be provided all the basic raw materials including cement, aggregate etc. on the spot only. No admixtures and other additives will be provided; participants can bring their own material (if they require).
Team Size: 2 - 4 members
Event Judging Criteria: Floatation test, Loading, Design of canoe, Load weight ratio, Aesthetic Value, Design mix proportion

Draw, Bid and Sell

RULES AND REGULATION-
 Each team shall have a maximum of 4 people.
Round-I:
 Each team has to undergo a technical MCQ written quiz of 30 questions focusing on Concrete technology, Surveying, Rocks and Geology and strength of materials.
 On the basis of written MCQ’s 6 teams will get through Round-I.
Round-II:
 Round-II will be a visual round related to civil engineering.
 3 visuals will be given to each team. As per the situations students have to answer questions.
 Top 4 teams will qualify for next and final round.
Round-III:
 This round will start in ED hall.
 In this round the participants will be given some specification under which they will have to study the specification and draw the plan, elevation and section as per the specifications.
 After completion of drawing participants will fix a virtual amount for their architectural drawings.
Judgement Criteria:
 A fixed virtual amount will be given to judges for bidding.
 The winner team will be the one whose drawing will be sold with highest price.
 Decisions declared by judges will be final.

I. TECHNICAL TERMS (TIME LIMIT : 1 MINUTE)
